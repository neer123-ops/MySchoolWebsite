Campus Life

CAMPUS

Uniting utility with beauty, the picturesque Modern School with exposed brickwork, arched openings, circular windows and long corridors lined with verdant greens are a delight every morning for students and teachers. The natural lighting and open spaces are natural pathways for cool fresh air which keep the classrooms well-ventilated throughout the day. The management and architects understood how the atmosphere of a campus influences a childâ€™s attitude to her school. The 9 acre campus affords large spaces for track and field activities, lawn tennis, badminton, basketball, soccer, and, also has an olympic size swimming pool.

TRACK AND FIELD

This the place where students can prepare for races and play various sports on field.

SWIMMING POOL

Here students learn swimming and also prepare for inter-school swimming competitions

SENIOR LIBRARY

This library has so many interesting fictional and non-fictional books about all subjects.

AUDITORIUM

It is one of the best and important places in Modern School Vasnat Vihar. Many inter-school competitions are held here in our own school including annual functions, functions for the retirement of teachers, important speeches about the world etc.

CAFETERIA

Here every student can get tasty meals during the recess/break time.

CLINIC

This clinic is also one of the most important places in the school. Students of all ages can come here to get themsleves medicated if they get hurt badly or get injuries. Suitable suggestions are given. This place keeps the entire acadamey safe.

LABS

Here the students experiment with different kinds of scientific objects and learn science and other subjects using real life examples.
